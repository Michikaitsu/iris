Frequently Asked Questions about IRIS
Quick answers to common questions

IRIS (Intelligent Rendering & Image Synthesis) is a Stable Diffusion-based image generation system that allows you to create high-quality images from text descriptions.

It combines powerful AI models with an intuitive interface to make AI art generation accessible to everyone.

Minimum: GTX 1650 with 4GB VRAM (slow, 5-6 minutes per image)

Recommended: RTX 3060 with 12GB VRAM (2 minutes per image)

High-End: RTX 4090 with 24GB VRAM (30 seconds per image)

CPU-only mode is available but very slow (30-60 minutes per image).
