Prompt Engineering

Master the art of writing effective prompts
Turn your ideas into stunning AI art

üìù Prompt Structure

Basic Template:
[Subject] + [Style] + [Quality] + [Details]

1. Subject (What)

The main focus: "a warrior", "mountain landscape", "futuristic city"

2. Style (How it looks)

Art style: "digital art", "oil painting", "photorealistic", "anime"

3. Quality Tags

Modifiers: "masterpiece", "highly detailed", "8k", "professional"

4. Technical Details

Lighting, composition: "dramatic lighting", "bokeh", "wide angle"

‚ú® Effective Examples

Portrait Photography

"close-up portrait of a woman, studio lighting, bokeh background, professional photography, highly detailed, 8k, photorealistic"

Good for: Professional headshots, character portraits

Cyberpunk Scene

"cyberpunk city street at night, neon lights, rain puddles reflecting lights, blade runner style, cinematic composition, highly detailed, digital art"

Good for: Sci-fi environments, atmospheric scenes

Fantasy Landscape

"magical forest with glowing mushrooms, misty atmosphere, ethereal lighting, fantasy art, concept art, artstation trending, highly detailed"

Good for: Fantasy worlds, concept art

Character Design

"full body character design of a medieval knight, ornate armor, dramatic pose, white background, concept art, character sheet, highly detailed"

Good for: Game characters, character concepts

üö´ Negative Prompts

Tell the AI what you DON'T want. Essential for quality results.

Common Negative Prompt:
"low quality, worst quality, blurry, distorted, ugly, deformed, disfigured, bad anatomy, wrong anatomy, extra limbs, missing limbs, floating limbs, watermark, text, error, cropped"

Quality Issues

low quality, worst quality, blurry, pixelated

Anatomy Issues

bad anatomy, extra fingers, missing limbs, deformed

Artifacts

watermark, text, signature, error, cropped

‚ö†Ô∏è Common Mistakes

Too Vague

"a person"
"close-up portrait of a young woman with blue eyes, studio lighting, professional photography"

Conflicting Styles

"realistic anime pixel art photograph"
"anime style portrait, highly detailed" OR "photorealistic portrait"

Missing Negative Prompts

Always include negative prompts to avoid common artifacts and quality issues.

üíé Pro Tips

1. Emphasis with Parentheses

Use (word:1.2) to increase emphasis

"(highly detailed:1.3) portrait"

2. Artist References

Reference specific artists for style

"in the style of Artgerm"

3. Platform References

Use art platforms for quality

"trending on artstation"

4. Camera/Lens Terms

Technical photography terms

"50mm lens, f/1.4, bokeh"
